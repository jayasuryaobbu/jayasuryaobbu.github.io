Nalugu (Haldi)

Nalugu is a traditional Hindu pre-wedding ritual commonly performed in South Indian weddings. It takes place separately for the bride and groom at their respective homes. During this ceremony, a mixture of turmeric, oil, and other auspicious items is applied to the bride and groom's skin to cleanse and beautify them before the wedding day.

Mehndi

The mehndi event is a traditional pre-wedding ritual in Hindu marriages, commonly held in India. During this ceremony, intricate designs are applied to the bride's hands and feet using henna paste. It is considered a symbol of good luck, love, and beauty, and the event often includes celebration with family and friends.

The pre-wedding party in Hindu marriages, is a joyful and vibrant celebration held a day or two before the wedding ceremony. The W E D D I N G Day

The wedding ceremony in Hindu marriages, known as the "Vivaham" or "Shaadi," is a sacred and elaborate affair that involves various rituals and traditions. The ceremony usually takes place in front of a sacred fire (Agni) and is conducted by a Hindu priest, known as a Pandit. The couple takes vows, exchanges garlands, and performs rituals like "Saptapadi" (seven sacred steps) around the fire, symbolizing their commitment to each other and their journey through life together.

Satyanarayana Swamy Vratham (Prayers to God)

The Satyanarayana Swamy Vratham is a significant Hindu ritual commonly performed by families to seek the blessings of Lord Vishnu, specifically in the form of Lord Satyanarayana. In general, it can be performed on auspicious occasions like weddings for divine blessings and prosperity. The vratham involves a detailed puja with offerings of fruits, flowers, and prasadam, and it is usually followed by a feast shared with family and friends.

Post Wedding-Party

The post-wedding party or reception in Hindu marriages is a grand celebration held after the wedding ceremony to commemorate the newly married couple and introduce them to the extended family and friends. It is a festive occasion filled with music, dance, and sumptuous feasting. The couple receives blessings and good wishes from the guests, and there may be speeches, performances, and games to entertain everyone.
